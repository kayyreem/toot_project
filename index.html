TootAfrica is GovTech agency Amplifying the Commuter voice.

Our Mission is to make road-safety everyone's business, anytime, everywhere.


a State of Emergency on South Africa's Roads!

Commuters Globally & SA in particular, are impacted by an alarming rate of accidents, caused by behaviorial factors (i.e. excessive speeding, distracted & intoxicated driving, intoxicated pedestrians, jay-walking etc) consequently causing fatalities on their daily commute to school, work or enroute to destinations of leisure, which poses a significant threat to safety & securuity of communities.

Of the 10 466 recorded fatal crashes in 2022, this resulted in 12 436 fatailties, which is an average of 34-lives lost on the roads daily. From an economic stand-point, the burden on the SA economy cost approximately 3.5% of GDP, valued at R198.79 Billion (2021/22) RTMC 2022/23 Anuual Report in an Economy that grew by 1.9% in 2022.

The Toot-way

Our primary goal is to meaningfully contribute a technological approach towards decreasing Road-Fatalities across African roads with the various road-users.
Our secondary objectives forms part of efforts towards democratizing access to digital transport-management solution, where the Commuters use technology to "Toot-out" dangerous road-behaviors, unsafe vehicles & situations that compromise their safety, as passengers, pedestrians or by-standers, to mitigate behavior-related incidents, before they become consequences of frustrations, like road-rage, intolerance, accidents & worst-case, fatalities incurred on our roads.
Lastly, we aim to bolster the law enforcement agencies capacity to tackle non-compliant behaviors, be proactive in addressing belligerent drivers & intercept other reported incidents before they become accidents & fatalities, efficiently.

Our Software that will be the intermediary agency between the following road-users:

Civil Society:
  • Private vehicles (Motorised & Non-motorised), Tourists, Pedestrians, Public Transport Commuters & the Special needs community (physical & hearing)
Industry & Business
  • Secondary Market (Parts suppliers) & Tertiary providers: Mechanic-services, Fleet managers, Insurance providers, Last-mile delivery services.
Public sector:
  • Transport & Fleet Operators
Law enforcement agencies:
  • Municipal/Regional & National Traffic with mandates of crime-prevention, combating, visibile policing & enforcement functions.

The long term goal is to feed into an advanced transport management system that provides real-time insights on how different Road-Users ie Commuters, Transport Operators, Commercial, Private & Law enforcements, interact transparently within the Transport ecosystem.
By leveraging community generated data & geo-spacial tags we'll be able to generate new insight identifying hotspots, bottlenecks, trends regarding when, how & where these frustrating incidents & crime occur, and how they contribute towards the fatalties on the roads. Identifying infrastructure deficiencies & advocating on infrastructure developments based open-access credible data.
