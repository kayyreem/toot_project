Toot Africa (Global), making road safety, everyone's business, anytime & everywhere.

Our goal is to amplify the Commuter voice.


Commuters Globally & SA in particular, are impacted by an alarming rate of accidents, caused by belligerent behavior (i.e. reckless, distracted & intoxicated driving, uninsured drivers etc) consequently causing fatalities on their daily commute to school, work or enroute to destinations of leisure, which pose a significant threat to transport safety & communities. Looking specifically South Africa, a significant burden placed on the SA economy 3.29% GDP, valued at approximately R198 Billion (2021/22) in an Economy that grew by 1.9% in 2022.

The Toot-way

Our proposal is a A digital transport safety solution, where users use technology to "Toot" wreckless drivers out, instead of getting involved in road rage or physical confrontations.

Our Software that will serve as an intermediary agency between the following market participants/users:
Commuters: Private vehicle owners, (Motorised & Non-motorised), Cyclers, tourists, students, public transport, disabled persons (physical & hearing), elderly persons
Commercial Automotive sector: Secondary (Parts suppliers) & Tertiary providers (Mechanic, Fleet managers, Last-mile delivery services, e-Hailing services).
Public sector: Transport & Fleet Operators
Law enforcement agencies: Coordinate local, regional & national response police & traffic responses

The long term goal is to feed into an advanced transport management system that can provide real-time insights on how Road Users with different interests ie Commuters, Transport Operators, Commercial, Private & Law enforcements, engage transparently in the Transport ecosystem, to transpose with incidents over geo-spacial data to identify hotspots bottlenecks, frustrating incidents & correlate crime with infrastructure deficiencies & advocating on infrastructure developments based on credible data.
