TootAfrica is GovTech agency Amplifying the Commuter voice.

Our Mission is to make road-safety everyone's business, anytime, everywhere.


There's a State of Emergency on South Africa's Roads!

Commuters Globally & SA in particular, are impacted by an alarming rate of accidents, caused by belligerent behaviorial factors (i.e. reckless, distracted & intoxicated driving, underage drivers etc) consequently causing fatalities on their daily commute to school, work or enroute to destinations of leisure, which pose a significant threat to transport safety & communities. Looking specifically South Africa, a significant burden placed on the SA economy 3.29% GDP, valued at approximately R198 Billion (2021/22) in an Economy that grew by 1.9% in 2022.

The Toot-way

Our primary goal is to meaningfully contribute towards decreasing Road-Fatalities across African roads. Our secondary objectives forms part of an open-access digital transport-management solution, where a community of users use technology to "Toot-out" wreckless driving-behaviors, unsafe vehicles & situation that compromised their individual & collective safety, to mitigate behavior-related incidents, before they become bigger consequences of frustrations, like road-rage, intolerance, accidents & worst-case, fatalities incurred on our roads.

Our Software that will be the intermediary agency between the following market participants/users:

Civil Society:
  • Private vehicles (Motorised & Non-motorised), Tourists, Students, Public Transport, Special needs community (physical & hearing) & the Elderly community.
Business & Industry
  • Secondary Market (Parts suppliers) & Tertiary providers: Mechanic-services, Fleet managers, Insurance providers, Last-mile delivery services.
Public sector:
  • Transport & Fleet Operators
Law enforcement agencies:
  • Municipal/Regional & National Traffic with mandates of crime-prevention, combating, visibile policing & enforcement functions.

The long term goal is to feed into an advanced transport management system that provides real-time insights on how different Road-Users ie Commuters, Transport Operators, Commercial, Private & Law enforcements, interact transparently within the Transport ecosystem. Leveraging on community generated data, over geo-spacial data, identifying hotspots, bottlenecks, trends regarding when, how & where these frustrating incidents & crime occur, and how they contribute towards the fatalties on the roads. Identifying infrastructure deficiencies & advocating on infrastructure developments based open-access credible data.

We've included a link to the latest Medium-Term-Strategic_Framework-2019/24 a 5-year document outlining the implementation plan of the 2030 NDP goals. It's a supporting document, highlighting the need of a collaborative & technological effort towards achieving some of the key UN SDG's, AU goals & NRSS goals, in decreasing the Fatalities on roads by 50% in 2030, supported by various policy instruments.
